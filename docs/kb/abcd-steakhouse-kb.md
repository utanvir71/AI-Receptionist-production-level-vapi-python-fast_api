# ABCD Steakhouse Gangnam Knowledge Base

## Branch Information

Branch name: ABCD Steakhouse Gangnam

ABCD Steakhouse is a fictional restaurant identity used for this public demonstration.

## Location

Address: 8F, 123 Demo-ro, Gangnam-gu, Seoul, South Korea

Nearest subway: Gangnam Station Exit 8, about 300 meters away.

Directions: Leave Gangnam Station through Exit 8, walk straight for two blocks, and enter ABCD Demo Tower on the right. The restaurant is on the 8th floor.

## Business Hours

Business hours may change on holidays.

- Every day: 11:00 to 21:00.
- Kitchen closes and last order is 20:00.
- Break time: 16:00 to 17:00.
- Reservation start times are accepted from 11:00 to 14:30 and from 17:00 to 19:30.
- Reservation start times are not accepted from 14:31 to 16:59.

Holiday and long-weekend hours may vary, so guests should confirm before visiting.

## Parking

Parking is available underground in the same building.

- The first 2 hours are free.
- After 2 hours, parking costs 1,000 KRW per 10 minutes.

## Safe Response Guidance

### Seating Requests

We can take a seating request, but exact seat assignment, including window-side seats, may vary depending on same-day availability. Offer to leave a note for the guest's preferred seating type.

### Maximum Group Size

Up to 12 guests is usually possible. For 13 or more guests, seating must be confirmed first.

### Late Arrivals and No-Shows

If guests are late for a reservation, seating may be adjusted depending on the store situation. Ask guests to call the restaurant if they think they will be late.

### Walk-Ins

Walk-ins are welcome. However, there may be a waiting time if the restaurant is fully booked.
